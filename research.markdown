#### **Current Projects**<br>
I am currently working on researching universal and optimal methods for composite minimization. Specifically, we want to allow for heterogeneous components that vary in their level of smoothness (from standard *L*-smooth with Lipschitz gradients to nonsmooth Lipschitz functions themselves). We also are looking for where we can benefit from varying levels of convexity in the components.
