\item Suppose $f$ is a root of $f(x)$ with $f''(r) = 0$. Derive the convergence rate of Newton's method in this case near the root $r$. Your answer cannot be that the error is 0.

\begin{solution}
$$e_{k+1} = \frac{f'''(r)}{3!f'(r)} e_k^3$$

Cubic convergence (order 3).
\end{solution}
