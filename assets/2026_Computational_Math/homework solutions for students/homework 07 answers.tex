          \begin{solution}
              \textbf{(a)} By MVT applied to $f$ on $[t_k, x]$, there exists $s \in (t_k, x)$ such that:
              $$f(x) = y_k + (x - t_k)f'(s)$$

              \textbf{(b)} $p_n(x) = y_k + (x - t_k)\dfrac{y_{k+1} - y_k}{t_{k+1} - t_k}$.

              By MVT applied to $f$ on $[t_k, t_{k+1}]$, there exists $u \in (t_k, t_{k+1})$ such that $f'(u) = \dfrac{y_{k+1} - y_k}{t_{k+1} - t_k}$.

              By MVT applied to $f'$ on $[s, u]$, there exists $v \in (t_k, t_{k+1})$ such that:
              $$f'(s) - \frac{y_{k+1} - y_k}{t_{k+1} - t_k} = (s - u)f''(v)$$

              \textbf{(c)} Combining parts (a) and (b):
              $$f(x) - p_n(x) = (x - t_k)(s - u)f''(v)$$

              Since $x, s, u \in (t_k, t_{k+1})$, both $|x - t_k| < h$ and $|s - u| < h$, so:
              $$\|f - p_n\|_\infty \leq Mh^2$$
          \end{solution}
