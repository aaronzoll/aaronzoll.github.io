Unit-width corridors; turn angle ϕ=π−α. At α=90° this is Gerver's sofa (area ≈ 2.2195). The sofa is drawn in a fixed corridor; as it turns, its pose is Tt(S)=R−t(S−x(t)), which provably stays inside the walls for all t. This is the locally-optimal (stationary) shape, not a proven global optimum.
